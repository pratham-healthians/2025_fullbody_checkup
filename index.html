Whats included in Full Body Checkup

A full body checkup typically includes a wide range of tests to assess your overall health. These cover essential blood investigations such as Complete Blood Count (CBC), Hemoglobin, Blood Sugar (Fasting & Postprandial), Lipid Profile for cholesterol levels, as well as Liver Function and Kidney Function tests. Hormonal and metabolic health is evaluated through Thyroid Profile (T3, T4, TSH), Vitamin D, Vitamin B12, and Calcium levels.

Who should get a Full Body Checkup

A full body checkup is not just for people who are unwell — it’s a proactive step to stay healthy. Regular preventive checkups help in detecting hidden health conditions early, reduce long-term risks, and give you peace of mind about your overall well-being.

  • Adults above 30 years
  • Working professionals with busy or stressful lifestyles
  • People with a family history of diabetes, heart disease, or hypertension
  • Individuals facing fatigue, stress, irregular sleep, or unhealthy habits
  • Anyone who wants preventive care and early detection of health issues
